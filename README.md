**SwiftTab: The Ultimate All-in-One Productivity Hub for Google Chrome**

Are you tired of juggling dozens of tabs, losing important links, and switching between five different apps just to manage your daily workflow? SwiftTab is a premium, beautifully designed Chrome Extension engineered to completely unify your digital workspace. 

By replacing fragmented tools with a single, high-performance dashboard, SwiftTab transforms your browser into an intelligent productivity powerhouse.

### 🚀 Core Features

**📂 Advanced Bookmark & Group Manager**
Say goodbye to messy bookmark bars. Organize your links into custom "Groups" with our intuitive interface. Need to find something fast? Our proprietary 3-Tier Intelligent Search Engine instantly finds what you are looking for by intelligently prioritizing exact matches and prefixes, rendering results in milliseconds.

**🌅 Automated Startup Routines**
Stop manually opening the same 5 tabs every morning. With SwiftTab’s Startup Routine engine, you can designate a specific Bookmark Group to automatically launch the second you open Chrome. Set it to run daily, only on weekdays, or on a specific date to perfectly match your workflow.

**⏰ Intelligent Task Scheduler & Reminders**
Never miss a meeting, deadline, or live event again. Schedule any URL to automatically open in a new tab at a specific date and time. Need a daily standup link to open every morning? Set a recurring schedule! Our robust, fail-safe background engine even includes a "Missed-Task Catchup" protocol—if you had your browser closed during a deadline, SwiftTab will instantly catch you up the second you return.

**📋 Universal Session Clipboard**
Your browser now has its own memory. SwiftTab automatically intercepts text you copy (Ctrl+C) across the web and stores it in your secure local Clipboard tab. See an image you need to save? Simply right-click it and select "Save Image to SwiftTab Clipboard" to instantly capture it. Easily review, copy, or delete your history all in one place.

**🧮 AppHub & Calculator**
Built right into your dashboard is a powerful suite of utilities. Run complex mathematical formulas securely inside our isolated sandbox environment, or use our dedicated Financial and usefull Calculators to instantly compute:
- EMI (Equated Monthly Installments)
- Loan Payments & Interest
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) Returns
- Compound Interest
- And More ...

### ✨ Why Choose SwiftTab?
* **Lightning Fast:** Built on Chrome's latest Manifest V3 architecture for zero-lag performance.
* **Privacy First:** Your data never leaves your computer. Everything (including your clipboard and schedules) is stored securely in your local browser memory.
* **Beautiful UI:** A stunning, modern interface featuring smooth micro-animations, glassmorphism, and a seamless Dark/Light mode toggle to protect your eyes at night.
