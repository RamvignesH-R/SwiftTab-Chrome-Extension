# Privacy Policy for SwiftTab

**Last Updated:** June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SwiftTab ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information. By installing and using SwiftTab, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
SwiftTab is designed with strict privacy at its core. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on any remote servers.** 

All data processed by SwiftTab is stored **100% locally** on your device using your browser's native `chrome.storage.local` API. This includes:
- Your saved bookmarks and folder structures.
- Your scheduled tasks and alarms.
- Your clipboard history (text and images you copy).
- Your UI theme and settings preferences.

## 2. Permissions & Data Usage
To function, SwiftTab requests specific browser permissions. This data never leaves your computer:
- **Web History & Bookmarks:** Used exclusively to display your most visited sites and manage your bookmarks within the extension's dashboard.
- **Website Content & User Activity:** Used exclusively by the local Clipboard Manager to temporarily store text or images you explicitly copy (via Ctrl+C or Right-Click). 

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing
Because we do not transmit or collect your data, **we do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties under any circumstances.** Your data is yours, and it remains completely confined to your local hard drive.

## 4. User Control and Data Deletion
You have complete control over your data. Because all data is stored locally:
- You can manually clear your clipboard history or completed tasks directly within the extension UI.
- If you uninstall SwiftTab from your browser, all data associated with the extension is permanently and automatically deleted from your hard drive by Google Chrome.

## 5. Changes to this Policy
If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy (for example, if a future feature requires a backend server), we will update this document and notify users through the extension's update notes.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please submit an issue on the "Support" tab in the Chrome Web Store.
